About the Artist

Narendra Panchal aka Nandu started painting limestone tiles at the age of 7 with broomsticks and lime. Without any formal education, he taught himself about deities, philosophy, and modern art. Living in Kerala jungles for 15 years, he is currently creating art for masses. Join him on his artistic journey.

Art Style

Mostly his art is inclined towards abstraction, but he can get into your thoughts to create a whole new experience on canvas, sheets, and wall. Be it acrylic, pastel, oil, water, he can even paint with the lime in your pocket (if you have it!)
